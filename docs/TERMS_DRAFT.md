# ModMirror Terms Draft

Last updated: 2026-05-21

Status: draft for review before public publishing. This is not legal advice and
should be reviewed before being used as the public app details link.

## What ModMirror Is

ModMirror is a Reddit Devvit moderation governance app. It helps moderator
teams identify enforcement drift, define rule policies, receive consistency
nudges, and review exceptions.

ModMirror is a consistency workflow for human moderators. It is not an
automated moderator, legal advice, a fairness guarantee, or a replacement for
moderator judgment.

## Human Confirmation

ModMirror does not automatically ban users by default. Meaningful enforcement
actions require moderator confirmation. Current delivery-sensitive and
destructive paths remain disabled or gated unless they have dedicated runtime
proof and explicit moderator approval.

## Attribution Limits

ModMirror uses deterministic matching and confidence labels. Historical
moderation records may be incomplete or ambiguous, so ModMirror may infer likely
rule attribution but does not guarantee perfect attribution.

Moderator teams remain responsible for reviewing recommendations, deciding
whether to apply policies, and following their community rules and Reddit's
policies.

## Acceptable Use

Use ModMirror only for legitimate subreddit moderation workflows. Do not use it
to harass users, target individual moderators, create cross-subreddit
surveillance, evade Reddit policies, automate punitive actions without review,
or publish private moderator data outside the appropriate moderation context.

## Disabled Or Unverified Capabilities

The current build keeps these paths disabled, preview-only, or unverified until
dedicated proof and approval exist:

- live public comment delivery;
- private message, modmail, or Mod Discussion delivery;
- native Mod Notes writes;
- scheduler jobs;
- real retention deletion against operational records;
- live Reddit remove/approve/ignore-reports execution;
- external AI calls;
- native Reddit mobile proof;
- true non-mod and lower-permission account runtime proof.

## Data And Privacy

Use of ModMirror is subject to the ModMirror privacy policy draft and Reddit's
applicable terms and policies. Moderator teams should review the privacy policy
before installing or publicly publishing the app.

## Reddit Platform Terms

ModMirror is built on Reddit's Developer Platform and must comply with Reddit's
Developer Terms, Devvit Rules, and other applicable Reddit Terms and Policies.
If Reddit platform terms conflict with this draft, Reddit's terms control.

## Changes And Availability

ModMirror may change as runtime proof, Devvit platform APIs, and moderator
needs evolve. Features may be removed, disabled, or kept in preview mode if they
cannot be operated safely.

## Contact

For questions, support, or privacy requests, contact the app owner through the
Reddit account or support route shown on the public app listing.
