Add optional delivery of a digest to the mod team if supported and verified.

## Important

This is optional. Do not block Wave 9 if Devvit support is unavailable or unstable.

1. Research current Devvit SDK/API for mod discussion/modmail delivery.
2. Update RESEARCH.md with exact findings.
