# Non-Goals and Boundaries

## Absolute no-submission boundary

Do not perform:
- Devpost submission,
- final project write-up,
- public publishing,
- final demo video,
- final screenshot polish,
- final app listing polish,
- prize positioning,
- contacting communities.

Engineering expansion only.

## Safety boundaries

Do not:
- silently remove/approve/ban content,
- send public comments without explicit confirmation,
- send modmail/mod discussion messages without runtime proof and confirmation,
- expose per-mod blame analytics,
- store unnecessary personal data,
- claim AI is authoritative,
- auto-enforce AI output,
- hide demo fallback as live behavior.
