# Real-World Product Bar

Every continuation wave must make ModMirror more useful to a real mod team.

## Useful means

A moderator can:
- understand which rules are enforced inconsistently,
- apply a team policy during a real moderation decision,
- record why a policy deviation happened,
- generate evidence for future review,
- improve consistency over time,
- discuss/adopt policies with other moderators,
- avoid unsafe automated actions.

## Should feel like

- moderation infrastructure,
- policy enforcement cockpit,
- evidence ledger,
- team decision system,
- safety-first assistant.

## Should not feel like

- generic SaaS dashboard,
- demo-only analytics toy,
- AI moderation black box,
- pile of cards with fake metrics.

## Judge-quality moments

1. Operational: apply policy from real post/comment context and produce a receipt.
2. Proof: show before/after drift improvement after policy adoption.
3. Review: generate evidence board/case packet that makes disputed moderation review easier.
